¿WHAT IS "MUR"?

"Make Your Routine" or "MUR" is a Web Page where you can introduce your goals and choose some options,then, we will make a routine for you, viewing your ideas and your stadistics, skills, goals, etc we will determinate what exercises are good for you and seeing the time you have to work out we will organize it to be comfortable for you.

¿HOW "MUR" WORKS?

You can choose beetwen, calisthenics and gym, and in manual mode you can make and edit your routine and save it in your profile, you can make as many routines as you want and we will make the changes that you need if you have an injury, disability or just some exercise make you feel bad.

¿WHO CAN USE "MUR"?

"MUR" was made for every want who want to start or just make a change in its life, because you can also choose for your level and we will make it harder or easier, using progressions in the weight, intensity and volume of your training.
